MILO ARNERICH-HATCH

CANVAS-EXPERIMENTS

Born and raised in NYC's East Village, Milo has been solving complex creative problems since grade school. He was first introduced to programming early on through Microworlds, animation with Flash and later to 3D Design with Maya and Rhino. While studying Electro-Mechanical Engineering (with the intention of building some cool robots) at CUNY City-Tech he was reintroduced to programming, realizing he enjoyed it alot. After becoming disillusioned by what "being an electro-mechanical engineer" really means, Milo began to rekindle his artistic roots using previously learned techniques in addition to the new, more technical ones.
